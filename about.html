Our Story

Invara Digital was founded in 2019 by a team of former Big Four security consultants who recognised that UK SMEs and mid-market companies were underserved by the cyber security industry. Too often, these businesses were forced to choose between expensive enterprise solutions or inadequate consumer-grade protection.

We set out to bridge this gap, offering enterprise-grade security services tailored to the specific needs and budgets of growing UK businesses. Based in Edinburgh, we maintain a strict UK-only policy‚Äîall our operations, data storage, and staff remain on British soil.

Today, we protect over 500 UK businesses, from fintech startups to established healthcare providers, helping them navigate an increasingly complex threat landscape while maintaining compliance with UK and international regulations.
